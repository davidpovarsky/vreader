## Observations

- `vreader-debug://present?sheet=toc&tab=contents` works exactly as Bug #253
  advertised — it sets the same `annotationsRoute` the chrome Contents button
  sets, so the presented `TOCSheet` is the real one with the real
  `currentLocator`. This is what makes the highlight observable CU-free.
- EPUB was used (not the original TXT repro) because CU-free navigation to a
  mid-book TXT position is blocked in the current harness: `search` times out
  waiting for the bundled `war-and-peace.txt` FTS index to come online
  (`search observer: timed out waiting for search index`, 30s budget), and
  `open?bookId=…&position=…` parses the position but the seek is deferred
  ("Seek dispatch deferred to feature #50" in `RealDebugBridgeContext.open`).
  EPUB indexed fine and the search-navigate landed Chapter Two. The
  `currentLocator` threading + `TOCContentsRow` styling are format-agnostic
  shared code, and the TXT `charOffsetUTF16` matching is unit-pinned.
- The TXT-search-index timeout is a pre-existing harness limitation worth a
  follow-up if TXT-specific visible verification is ever needed; not a Bug #248
  regression.
