## Observations

- The PDF fixture seeds + opens; the reader renders the 6-page document with a
  "Page 1 of 6" indicator (criterion 7 visible).
- Theme criterion: `PDFViewBridge` flips the **gutter** background (Bug #198,
  `PDFViewBridge.swift:43`), and the snapshot confirms light→dark. The white
  *page interior* does NOT invert — by design: Bug #198 explicitly scoped
  page-color inversion as a **separate feature**, not part of #17. So criterion
  8 is met as implemented + unit-tested.

## Out of scope / residual (CU-gated)

- **Criterion 1's UI gesture path**: creating a PDF highlight requires a real
  long-press + drag text selection on the `PDFView`. There is no DebugBridge
  PDF-highlight driver (the `highlight?start=&end=` command is UTF-16 / TXT-MD
  only — PDF highlights are page+rect based), and the gesture cannot be
  synthesized CU-free. The selection→annotation LOGIC is unit-verified over a
  real PDFDocument; only the gesture wiring is unexercised. Needs CU / a real
  device, OR a future `vreader-debug://pdf-highlight?page=&rect=` driver.

Row stays `DONE` (partial) — not flipped to `VERIFIED` — pending the
gesture-driven highlight on device.
