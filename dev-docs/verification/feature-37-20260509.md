## Observations

- The `Toggle("Custom settings for this book")` at `ReaderSettingsPanel.swift:491` only renders when `bookFingerprintKey != nil` (line 56's gate). For Position Test Book, `bookFingerprintKey` is set, so the section appears as expected.
- **CU+iOS Sim Toggle quirk** (already noted in feature #34's round-3 evidence as "external CU/Sim infra quirk, not filed as bug"): a single `left_click` at the toggle's center does NOT flip a SwiftUI `Toggle` on the booted iOS 26.4 simulator. A short `left_click_drag` of ~13 pixels mimics the iOS swipe-to-toggle gesture and reliably flips it. This is a verification-tooling quirk, not a vreader defect.
- The subtitle text at `ReaderSettingsPanel.swift:497-499` correctly reflects the toggle state (uses `isPerBookEnabled` ternary). This is the user-facing confirmation that the toggle "did something" — the binding is one-way reactive, no save button needed.
- The `onChange` handler at line 493-495 calls `savePerBookSnapshot()` when toggling ON and `deletePerBookOverride()` when toggling OFF. Per-book persistence behaviour is cross-covered by the `perBookSettings_savesAndRestores` unit test cited from row 37.
- The "Custom Background" toggle (themed background image) in the same panel — and the broader sheet's many sections (theme / fontSize / lineSpacing / fontFamily / cjkSpacing / chineseConversion / tapZone / preview) are visible and laid out correctly; not exercised this round since #37's verification target was the per-book toggle specifically.
- No bugs observed.
