## Observations

- WI-12's EPUB Photo background image renders cleanly: the base64
  `data:` URL approach injects the photo into the EPUB WKWebView CSS
  with no `allowingReadAccessTo` dependency, and the Photo theme's
  translucent paper layer keeps the body text legible over it.
- The reader chrome over the Photo theme with a *dark* background
  photo is low-contrast — the Contents / AI labels are faint against a
  dark photo region (clear and legible on every flat theme, incl.
  OLED's true black). Not a regression and not a WI-12 artifact (the
  chrome is theme-tinted; a busy dark photo simply reduces contrast).
  Worth a future polish pass (a chrome scrim on the Photo theme) but
  out of scope for #60's acceptance — chrome renders and functions.
- Theme switching is live and immediate on the EPUB reader — the
  `vreader-theme` style element is located-and-replaced per WI-4.
- Pre-existing full-suite flake (`TTSServiceSpeedControlTests` /
  `BookSourceHTTPClientTests` parallel-runner crash) is unrelated to
  #60 — confirmed across WI-8/9/10/11/12 by isolated suite runs.
