# Feature #68 — Reader chapter-start typography — Gate-5 round 2 (partial; EPUB drop-cap + per-theme now CU-free confirmed)

Follows the 2026-05-19 partial (`feature-68-20260519.md`), which device-verified
TXT fully but deferred the EPUB / MD / paged on-device **visuals** (blockers at
the time: "DebugBridge EPUB-open did not navigate", no MD fixture in
`DebugFixtureCatalog`, CU outage). This round re-attempts the deferred visuals
CU-free on v3.39.40.

**New this round:**
- A `mini-markdown` MD fixture now exists in `DebugFixtureCatalog` (the 2026-05-19
  "no MD fixture" blocker is gone).
- The EPUB drop-cap (#5) + per-theme recolor (#7) are now **definitively
  confirmed CU-free** via `eval` computed-style inspection of the live WKWebView
  — a stronger confirmation than a screenshot (it reads the rendered
  `::first-letter` style, not a visual guess), and it sidesteps the
  "EPUB-open-didn't-navigate" blocker entirely.

**Still blocked:** the MD drop-cap (#3) + paged-mode drop-cap (#9) visuals.
The native paged MD/TXT reader cannot be driven to the chapter-top page via the
harness — `open?position=0` lands at a stale offset (observed `snapshot.position
= 833` on a 925-byte doc, "Page 2 of 2", blank), and a `UITextView` has no
`eval` path. Filed as **Bug #272 / GH #1193** (the concrete unblock). So #68
stays `DONE` (partial), not `VERIFIED`.

## Observations

- The `eval` computed-style technique is the right CU-free verification for
  WKWebView-rendered drop-caps: it confirms the rule is *applied to the rendered
  DOM* (not merely injected), which the unit tests can't. The EPUB drop-cap +
  per-theme recolor are now solidly verified without CU and without needing the
  reader to scroll to the chapter top.
- The MD/TXT drop-cap can't use this technique (UITextView, no `eval`), and the
  harness can't drive the paged native reader to page 1 (Bug #272). That is the
  sole remaining blocker for #68 → VERIFIED. Once #272 ships, an MD/TXT
  page-1 screenshot closes #3 + #9.
- AZW3 #6 is a feature-correct safe-miss on the only AZW3 fixture; not a defect.
