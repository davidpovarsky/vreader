## Observations

- The headline behavior (Ask AI on a selection → Chat tab seeded with the exact
  selected text, not auto-sent) works end-to-end on device on the Readium engine,
  exercising the full router → `.readerAskAIRequested` → `ReaderAskAIReadObserver`
  → `pendingAskAIText` → `onChange(showAIPanel)` drain → `chatViewModel.seedInput`
  → `AIChatView.applySeedIfPossible` chain.
- Re-opening the AI panel after dismiss showed the **Summarize** (default) tab —
  confirming the dismiss tab-reset, not a stale `.chat`.
- TTS audio and the unconfigured-readiness path are not CU-observable / conflict
  with `--enable-ai`; both are unit + Gate-4-audit verified, and the Read action
  shares the mechanism device-proven by criterion 1.
